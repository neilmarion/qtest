What is HyperCube™?

The HyperCube™ is one of Quipper's most fascinating cube projects, combining facets of the standard square and cube, while allowing for a staggering n dimensions!

Imagine the possibilities!

Where the limits of standard cubes end, the endless possibilities of hypercubes begin! Prepare yourself to step into a whole new dimension.

How is it possible?

Since it is so difficult to imagine an n-dimensional object in a 3D space, let alone the flat plane of this very website, we will represent hypercubes as if they were standard cubes.
